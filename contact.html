Quick Answers

How do I register as a contestant?

Click the "Register" button at the top of any page and fill out the form. Registration is free and open to Embakasi residents aged 18-30.

When does voting start?

Voting begins on November 1, 2025 and runs through December 15, 2025. Each vote costs 20 KSH via M-Pesa.

Can I sponsor the event?

Yes! We offer various sponsorship packages. Contact our partnerships team via email or phone for details.

Where is the grand finale?

The grand finale will be held at Embakasi Stadium on December 31, 2025. Tickets will be available from December 1st.
