About Mr. & Miss Embakasi

Celebrating Talent, Beauty, and Community Spirit

Our Mission

Mr. & Miss Embakasi is dedicated to showcasing the vibrant talent and beauty within the Embakasi community. Our mission is to:

  • Empower young men and women to showcase their talents and leadership qualities
  • Promote community development through charitable initiatives
  • Celebrate the diversity and culture of Embakasi
  • Provide a platform for personal and professional growth

Our History

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by a group of community leaders, the first Mr. & Miss Embakasi competition was held with just 20 contestants.

2020 - Going Digital

Launched our online voting platform, allowing the entire community to participate in selecting their favorites.

2022 - Charity Focus

Introduced charity initiatives, with winners leading community projects that impacted over 5,000 residents.

2025 - Bigger Than Ever

Now with over 200 contestants annually and a grand finale attended by thousands at Embakasi Stadium.

Frequently Asked Questions

The competition is open to all residents of Embakasi aged 18-30. Contestants must provide proof of residence and a valid ID. Both men and women can participate in their respective categories.
Voting is done through our secure online platform. Each vote costs 20 KSH via M-Pesa. You can vote for multiple contestants, but only once per contestant per day. The voting period runs for 4 weeks leading up to the grand finale.
Winners receive cash prizes, modeling contracts, brand ambassador opportunities, and a chance to lead community development projects. The exact prizes vary each year but are always substantial and career-changing.
60% of proceeds go to organizing the event and prizes, 30% supports community projects in Embakasi, and 10% covers administrative costs. We publish annual financial reports for transparency.
